A signal commons for music

Music becomes a place when people hear it together.

Dotify turns a simple link into a shared listening moment, while artist-owned runtimes keep access, rights, and value under the artist's control.

The social core

One link. One moment. No ceremony.

The room is not a feature beside the player. It is the place where listening becomes relation and discovery happens through another person.

  1. Open a room

    The host chooses a work and opens a temporary listening space around it.

  2. Share the threshold

    The room link resolves its real host, work, and presence context before entry.

  3. Listen together

    A guest chooses a local room name and joins the WebRTC stream without wallet or key access.

Artist sovereignty

The platform should never swallow the artist.

Shared listening matters only if the people who make the work remain in control of how it circulates.

Catalog

A runtime that belongs to the artist

Dotify's active contract model gives each artist a personal SmartRuntime instead of placing every catalog decision inside one platform-owned contract.

Access

Doors the artist can define

Free and paid Classic access are explicit product policies. Human-free access remains future work until real, privacy-respecting Proof of Personhood is integrated.

Value

Rights and splits that can be audited

Runtime rules keep royalties, collaborator shares, and access decisions inspectable without making chain mechanics the listener's first experience.

Circulation

Presence without source-file distribution

A room guest hears the host's temporary media stream. Protected source references and content keys stay outside public room state.

Invisible trust

Infrastructure belongs in the guarantee.

Wallet signatures, encrypted delivery, WebRTC boundaries, and on-chain policy do real work behind the experience. They should make listening safer, not more bureaucratic.

Individual play
The backend verifies a signed request, resolves the artist runtime, and checks access before temporary key delivery.
Protected room
Access is checked against the host. Room guests receive the ephemeral WebRTC stream, never the protected source file or content key.
Publication
The source-level owner boundary and regression coverage exist, but production writes remain closed until the deployed registry path is remediated.
Production gate — publication is quarantined

The deployed registry facet and existing runtimes must be remediated and re-audited before artist publication is enabled. This page does not imply that an on-chain remediation has occurred. Read the operator runbook.

Production before spectacle

The calm surface needs a strong spine.

  1. Delivered in the repository

    Core access, upload, room, and test paths

    Server-side uploads and key delivery, wallet-signed access requests, hosted signaling, room links, and deterministic critical-flow tests form the current production spine.

  2. Current gate

    Remediate the live registry boundary

    The owner-safe facet, dry-run tooling, and operator plan exist. Deployment, owner-signed runtime upgrades, migration, and final audit still require explicit operational approval.

  3. Sequenced later

    Personhood and cultural transmission, only when real

    Proof of Personhood, consented provenance, and ambassador mechanics remain future work. Dotify will not represent them as active capabilities before their foundations exist.

A shared link is the front door. Artist sovereignty is the foundation.

Dotify is not a Spotify clone. It is a cultural social hub where music can reopen a commons between people without dispossessing the artist.
