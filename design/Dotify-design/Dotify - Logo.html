01

The mark

Five dots on a rhythmic grid, threaded by one smooth line. Geometric, reproducible at any size, and unmistakably “connect the dots”.

  • Dots sit at the peaks and valleys of the line — a melody you could hum.
  • One line, no breaks — the music never drops the connection.
  • The accent dot (top-right, green) is the resolving note — the “now playing”.
  • Built on an 9×9 unit grid; the line uses a single stroke weight for clean small-size rendering.
